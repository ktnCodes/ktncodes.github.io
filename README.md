# ktncodes.github.io

An embedded systems and agentic engineering notebook turned public. This blog blends technical deep-dives, debugging write-ups, and AI tooling notes with a focus on systems thinking, root-cause analysis, and shipping production-quality work.

## What You'll Find

- **Engineering notes**: C++/Qt, embedded Linux, real-time systems, and the hard bugs that come with them
- **Agentic engineering**: building with AI agents, GitHub Copilot workflows, and AI-assisted development in production
- **Debugging & systems**: SIGSEGV/SIGABRT crash analysis, concurrency defects, log-driven root-cause methodology
- **Tooling & process**: coding standards, onboarding docs, team-wide AI adoption, and repeatable workflows
- **Learning logs**: things I'm studying, synthesizing, and want to remember
- **Build-in-public**: honest reflections on what shipped, what broke, and what I'd do differently

## Writing Style

- Show your work: decisions, tradeoffs, failures, and how they were actually fixed
- Reproducible and specific — not hand-wavy, not high-level fluff
- Systems and leverage mindset: optimize for reliability and compounding knowledge
- Written for future-me and anyone debugging the same thing at 2am
