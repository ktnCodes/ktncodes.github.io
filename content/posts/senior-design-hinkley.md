## Overview

For my senior design project at Iowa State, our team built an internal infrastructure web application for Hinkley Medical, a small startup with a functional medical device product entering the market.

The problem: all shipment, inventory, and warranty tracking was done manually through Microsoft Excel. This was inefficient and didn't allow consumers to view or manage their own product information. Our application replaced this manual process with a proper web-based system.

## My Contribution

I worked as a frontend developer, creating designs and implementing functionality to make the application intuitive and accessible for Hinkley Medical employees.

## What I Learned

- JavaScript with React for frontend development
- HTML & CSS design patterns
- AWS Amplify for deployment
- Working with a real client on a production-bound product
