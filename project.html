BoomBa Project

Project Overview:

In general military and police practice. Breaking into buildings can be one of the most dangerous things that can be done while on the job. You do not know what is on the other side of the door. Enemies can be in places where you least expect it. The risk of breaking into these buildings and getting attacked in spots that are unknown is very dangerous. However, we are able to minimize these risks with the use of a robust robotic design, object detection sensors and simple and yet effective programming code. Our solution is to use a relatively common and out of the ordinary cleaning robot and make it a recon bot in order to scout in these buildings with relatively little risk. In addition if need be, there is a last resort option in case things get too messy.

Contibution:

I personally worked on implmented embedded coding for senors and automonous detection of objects on the field. I also was the lead for documentation of the project.
